I'm Adeyma — the teacher behind Adeyma Tutoring Time

I'm a TEFL/TESOL-certified English teacher, and I've been tutoring since 2023 — warm, patient one-to-one lessons, all online, for women, girls and young children.

I'm also studying for a BSc in Psychology, which shapes the way I teach: lessons are gentle, encouraging and built around how each person really learns. A shy or struggling learner is always met with calm and kindness.

To keep things fun, I use lively tools like Kahoot, Blooket, Wordwall and Canva — with clear goals, gentle practice and homework that genuinely helps. I also speak Dutch, French and Arabic, which comes in handy when a child needs a little support in another language.
